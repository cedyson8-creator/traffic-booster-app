## Features

### How often is my data updated?
Real-time data updates every 30 seconds. Forecasts and recommendations update hourly. Email reports are sent at scheduled times.

### Can I manage multiple websites?
Yes! Traffic Booster Pro supports unlimited websites. Connect multiple Google Analytics properties and switch between them easily.

### How far back does historical data go?
Up to 90 days of historical data is available. This is limited by Google Analytics API. For older data, export from Google Analytics directly.

### What does the forecasting feature do?
The AI analyzes your traffic patterns to predict future growth. It provides 30-day and 90-day forecasts with confidence levels to help you plan marketing campaigns.

### What are optimization recommendations?
Data-driven suggestions to improve your SEO, campaign performance, and overall website effectiveness. Recommendations are based on your actual analytics data.

### How do I export my data?
1. Tap Export tab
2. Tap "Create Schedule"
3. Choose format (CSV, JSON, HTML, PDF)
4. Set frequency (daily, weekly, monthly)
5. Enter email address
6. Save

Reports will be sent automatically at scheduled times.
