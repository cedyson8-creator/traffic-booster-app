# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 14, 2026**

## 1. Introduction

Traffic Booster Pro ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and website (collectively, the "Service").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Service.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

- **Account Information:** Name, email address, phone number, and account credentials
- **Social Media Account Information:** When you authorize us to connect your Facebook, Instagram, or TikTok accounts
- **Payment Information:** Billing address, payment method details (processed securely through Stripe)
- **Communication Data:** Messages, feedback, and support requests

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data:** Features used, pages visited, time spent, interactions with the Service
- **Location Data:** General location based on IP address (not precise GPS)
- **Analytics Data:** Crash reports, performance metrics, and usage statistics

### 2.3 Social Media Data

When you connect your social media accounts, we collect:

- **Facebook/Instagram:** Page insights, post metrics, audience demographics, engagement data
- **TikTok:** Video analytics, account metrics, follower data, engagement statistics
- **Data Scope:** Only data you authorize through OAuth permissions

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for:

- **Service Delivery:** Providing, maintaining, and improving the Service
- **Account Management:** Creating and managing your account
- **Analytics:** Tracking and analyzing usage patterns and trends
- **Communication:** Sending service updates, security alerts, and support messages
- **Billing:** Processing payments and managing subscriptions
- **Legal Compliance:** Complying with applicable laws and regulations
- **Security:** Detecting and preventing fraud and unauthorized access

## 4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:

- **Encryption:** All data transmitted over HTTPS with SSL/TLS encryption
- **Secure Storage:** Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
- **Access Control:** Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
- **Regular Audits:** Security assessments and vulnerability testing

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 5. Third-Party Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share information with:

- **Service Providers:** Third parties that assist in operating our Service (e.g., payment processors, analytics providers)
- **Legal Requirements:** When required by law or to protect our rights
- **Social Media Platforms:** As necessary to provide the Service (Facebook, Instagram, TikTok APIs)

## 6. Social Media Platform Compliance

Our use of social media APIs complies with each platform's terms:

- **Facebook/Instagram:** We comply with Meta's Platform Policy and Data Use Restrictions
- **TikTok:** We comply with TikTok's Developer Terms of Service
- **Data Usage:** We only use data for the purposes you authorize

## 7. Data Retention

- **Account Data:** Retained while your account is active
- **Social Media Data:** Cached for performance; refreshed regularly
- **Deleted Accounts:** Personal data deleted within 30 days of account deletion
- **Backup Data:** May be retained for up to 90 days for recovery purposes

## 8. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

- **Access:** Request a copy of your personal information
- **Correction:** Update or correct inaccurate information
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your account and associated data
- **Opt-Out:** Unsubscribe from marketing communications
- **Revoke Access:** Disconnect social media accounts at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@trafficboosterpro.com.

## 9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete the information promptly.

## 10. International Data Transfers

If you access the Service from outside the United States, your information may be transferred to, stored in, and processed in the United States or other countries. By using the Service, you consent to such transfers.

## 11. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to delete personal information
- Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information
- Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

## 12. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation:

- Right of access to your personal data
- Right to rectification of inaccurate data
- Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
- Right to restrict processing
- Right to data portability
- Right to object to processing
- Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

## 13. Cookies and Tracking Technologies
