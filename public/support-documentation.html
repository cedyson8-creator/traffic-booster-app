‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Account & Setup

Q: How do I reset my password?

Tap Settings ‚Üí Help & Support ‚Üí Reset Password. Enter your email and follow the link in the confirmation email.

Q: Can I use one account for multiple websites?

Yes! Traffic Booster Pro supports unlimited websites per account. Connect multiple Google Analytics properties and switch between them easily.

Q: How do I delete my account?

Tap Settings ‚Üí Help & Support ‚Üí Delete Account. Confirm your email. Your account and all data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

Data & Analytics

Q: Why is my data not showing?

First, verify your Google Analytics property is connected correctly. Data may take 24 hours to appear initially. Try refreshing the dashboard (pull down).

Q: How far back does historical data go?

Traffic Booster Pro displays up to 90 days of historical data. This is limited by Google Analytics API. For older data, export from Google Analytics directly.

Q: How often is data updated?

Real-time data updates every 30 seconds. Forecasts and recommendations update every hour. Email reports are sent at scheduled times.

Q: Can I export my data?

Yes! Tap Export tab ‚Üí Create Schedule. Choose format (CSV, JSON, HTML, PDF), frequency, and email. Reports will be sent automatically.

Notifications & Alerts

Q: How do I enable notifications?

Tap Alerts ‚Üí Preferences. Toggle notification types (performance, forecasting, optimization). Choose delivery methods (email, push, in-app).

Q: Why am I not receiving notifications?

Check: 1) Notification permissions in phone Settings, 2) Notification preferences in app, 3) Internet connection is stable, 4) Email address is correct.

Q: Can I customize alert thresholds?

Yes! In Alerts ‚Üí Preferences, set severity levels for different notification types. You'll only receive alerts matching your preferences.

Integrations

Q: Is my Google Analytics data secure?

Yes! We use OAuth authentication and never store your Google password. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Q: Can I connect multiple Meta Ads accounts?

Currently, one Meta Ads account per Traffic Booster Pro account. You can switch accounts in Settings ‚Üí Connected Accounts.

Q: What if I disconnect my Google Analytics?

Your app will stop receiving new data, but historical data remains. Reconnect anytime in Settings ‚Üí Connected Accounts.
