## ğŸš€ Getting Started

### First Time Setup
1. **Install the app** from Google Play Store
2. **Sign in** with your email
3. **Connect Google Analytics** - Grant permission to access your website data
4. **Connect Meta Ads** (optional) - Grant permission to access your ad campaigns
5. **View your dashboard** - Start monitoring your traffic in real-time

### Connecting Google Analytics
1. Tap **Settings** â†’ **Connected Accounts**
2. Tap **Connect Google Analytics**
3. Select your website property
4. Grant permissions
5. Your data will appear in the dashboard within seconds

### Connecting Meta Ads
1. Tap **Settings** â†’ **Connected Accounts**
2. Tap **Connect Meta Ads**
3. Select your ad account
4. Grant permissions
5. Your campaign data will appear in the dashboard

### Setting Up Notifications
1. Tap **Alerts** tab
2. Tap **Preferences**
3. Choose notification types (performance, forecasting, optimization)
4. Set severity levels
5. Choose delivery methods (email, push, in-app)

### Scheduling Reports
1. Tap **Export** tab
2. Tap **Create Schedule**
3. Choose report format (CSV, JSON, HTML, PDF)
4. Set frequency (daily, weekly, monthly)
5. Enter email address
6. Save schedule

## ğŸ”§ Troubleshooting

### App Won't Connect to Google Analytics
1. Verify your Google account has access to the property
2. Check internet connection
3. Sign out and sign back in
4. Reinstall the app if issue persists

### No Data Showing in Dashboard
1. Verify Google Analytics property is connected
2. Check that your website has recent traffic
3. Allow 24 hours for initial data sync
4. Try refreshing the dashboard (pull down)

### Notifications Not Working
1. Check notification permissions in Settings
2. Verify notification preferences are enabled
3. Check email address is correct
4. Ensure internet connection is stable

### App Crashes on Startup
1. Clear app cache in device settings
2. Restart your device
3. Reinstall the app
4. Contact support if issue persists
